Public Records Request Ideas

Use these prompts to uncover how automated surveillance tools operate in Ellensburg and who they impact.

Data Sharing

Ask for the current list of agencies, departments, or private vendors the city shares Flock data with, plus any memorandums of understanding that authorize the sharing.

Camera Locations

Request the GPS coordinates or street intersections for every camera by department, including the installation rationale and any proximity to schools, clinics, or public housing.

Cost + Funding

Seek invoices, contracts, or grant paperwork showing the total cost to city, county, and tax payers, broken down by hardware, software, maintenance, and renewal fees.

Usage Policies

Request all policies governing data retention, search protocols, hot-list management, and audit logs to verify compliance with state privacy laws.

Oversight + Complaints

Ask for oversight reports, disciplinary files, or complaint logs related to ALPR misuse or data breaches over the past five years.

Impact Analysis

Request any equity assessments or demographic breakdowns that show who is disproportionately stopped or flagged because of Flock alerts.

Completed Requests

Share wins with your neighbors so we can amplify findings and push for stronger safeguards.

  • September 2024 · Vendor Contracts
    Obtained Flock contract with detailed renewal costs and NDA clauses; currently informing budget testimony at city council.
